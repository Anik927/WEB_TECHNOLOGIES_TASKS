Lalmonirhat, Bangladesh

Lalmonirhat is a district located in the northern part of Bangladesh. It is known for its natural beauty, including lush green landscapes, rivers, and wetlands. The district is home to various wildlife species and offers opportunities for eco-tourism. Visitors can explore the serene environment, enjoy boat rides on the rivers, and experience the local culture and hospitality of the people in Lalmonirhat.
